How the popular film 'Terminator 2: Judgement Day' can be compared to the hit video game 'Among Us.'












Story Info

Popular terminator fans will know that the second movie of the franchise showcases a day of judgement. On this day, the characters are 'judged' and based on several factors, one of them is voted out. This can be directly compared to the hit video game, 'Among Us' as in this game, players will find the 'impostor' and in meetings will vote a suspected 'impostor' out.
